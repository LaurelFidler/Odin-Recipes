Spicy Fish

Blackened Salmon with Spices and Lemon

Grilled Spicy Salmon served with rice, lemons and butter

Ingredients

  • 1 Filet Salmon
  • 2 Tbs Chili Powder
  • 2 Tbs Onion Powder
  • 2 tps Mustard Powder
  • 1 tps Cayenne Powder
  • 1 tps Thyme
  • 1 tps Oregano
  • Canola Oil
  • 1 Lemon
  • Butter for serving
  • 1 cup dry rice

Cooking

  1. Start cooking rice.
  2. Mix all spices together.
  3. Brush Salmon with oil until coated. Pat Salmon with spice mix until coated evenly on both sides.
  4. Cook Salmon on flat grill or fry in pan until done, depending on thickness. Flip halfway through.
  5. Turn off heat. Add butter on top of salmon, letting it melt over.
  6. Serve Salmon over rice. Sqeeze lemon juice over salmon.

Done!
